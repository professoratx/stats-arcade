Does the CI include zero?

Connecting confidence intervals to statistical significance and p-values

How to use: Adjust the mean difference between two groups, the standard error, and the sample size per group. Watch the 95% CI shift and stretch. If it includes zero (the red dashed line), the difference is not significant. If it avoids zero entirely, it is significant.

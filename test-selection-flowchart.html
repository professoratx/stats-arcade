How do I choose the correct significance test?

Choosing the right statistical test comes down to two questions about your study design. This flowchart walks you through them.

How to use this tool: Answer two questions: First, are you comparing one group across conditions (same people) or multiple groups (different people)? Second, how many groups or conditions are involved? The flowchart highlights the correct test based on your answers.
How to interpret: One group measured multiple times requires a dependent t-test (two conditions) or RMANOVA (three or more conditions). Multiple independent groups require an independent t-test (two groups) or ANOVA (three or more groups).

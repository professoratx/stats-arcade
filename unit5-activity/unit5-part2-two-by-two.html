What you're looking at: A 2×2 table (also called a contingency table) is the foundation for every epidemiological measure in this unit. It organizes data from two groups across two outcomes. All four statistics — RR, OR, ARR, and NNT — are calculated from the four cells of this table. In the mock article you just read, the 2×2 table is: early amplification (55 improved, 45 did not) and waitlist (25 improved, 75 did not).

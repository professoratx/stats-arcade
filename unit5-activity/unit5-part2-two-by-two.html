In this tool, you'll explore the risk of lung cancer (the outcome) based on exposure to smoking (the risk factor). A 2×2 table organizes data from two groups (smokers vs. non-smokers) across two outcomes (developed lung cancer vs. did not). Every epidemiological statistic — RR, OR, ARR, and NNT — is calculated from the four cells of this table.

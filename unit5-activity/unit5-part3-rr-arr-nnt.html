In this tool, you'll explore whether a new upper-extremity rehab protocol improves outcomes for post-stroke patients compared to standard OT. The tool lets you move sliders to change the improvement rates for each group and instantly see how RR, ARR, and NNT respond. The key insight: a treatment can look impressive in relative terms (RR) while having a very different real-world impact (NNT).

Unit 5 Class Activity

Interpreting Epidemiological Studies

A guided walkthrough of RR, OR, ARR, and NNT using interactive tools

How this activity works You will read a mock journal article, rate your confidence, and then explore four interactive tools — answering questions along the way. At the end, you'll re-rate your confidence and download a personalized completion certificate to submit to Canvas. Work through the parts in order; each one builds on the last.
Best on a laptop or tablet. The interactive tools use sliders and tables that work best on a larger screen. If you must use a phone, rotate to landscape mode.
